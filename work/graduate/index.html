The Graduate Re-Brand & Logo Design

I developed this project after entering my creative brief and mock up for redesign of southern regional college, graduate restaurant. The organisers where looking for concept ideas based on the remodelling and renovation of the restaurants interior.

My design style at the time was heavy focused on modern minimalism design trends with muted or neutral colours and designs that I had seen other trendy restaurants from around the world implement in their own ways.

Initial Design Concepts

 

After considering the colour and font choices for the logo, I decided on lato for the font and a colour palette made up of shades of mute red, greys and black.

Which follow the SRC brand guidelines, these colours I choose portray the underlaying meaning and style of the logo. Additionally, red has been found to stimulate appetite and highlight the word graduate bringing attention toward the name. Yet not over power current SRC branding.

Design Implementation and Outcome

Overall, I was delighted the concept and design really added value to the client and overall branding throughout the restaurant. To then have the chance to use skills I learnt as I progressed in a different field to give back in different capacity.
